Duluth Trading Company HQ

Audio-Video Control System

Throughout this project, our team designed and developed the digital interfaces that control audio and video devices throughout a large corporate headquarters. The facility features fully integrated AV systems that will be used to support routine office operations, team building environments, social gathering spaces, and fitness areas.

I completed this project as a member of the team at Professional Audio Designs. I can't say enough about how talented that group is, or about how lucky I was to work with them.

I am interested in discussing human-centered design topics, and collaborating on research, interactive, or space making projects. To reach out, find me on Instagram or Linkedin.
