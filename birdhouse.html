Bird House

Performing Arts Center Audio-Video Controls

Throughout this project, our team designed and developed the digital interfaces that control audio and video devices throughout a new performing arts center. The facility features fully integrated AV systems that will be used to support educational lectures, theatrical productions, recitals, and special events. This case study showcases my team's design process in this work, however I have changed the organization's name and branding images to respect the privacy of the client.

I am interested in discussing human-centered design topics, and collaborating on research, interactive, or space making projects. To reach out, find me on Instagram or Linkedin.
